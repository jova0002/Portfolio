TOPIC

This topic provides a basic understanding of the interplay between users and user interfaces, as well as which theories, tools and methods you can use for research, design and digital product development. Understanding this interplay is important so that design choices and product development are based on concrete insights about real users instead of vague hunches and immediate assumptions

GOAL

The purpose of this theme is to give you experience with selectedUX/UI methods and to teach you how to present your product/solution design and development process and communicate your research and test results to stakeholders

PROCESS

Brainstorm & Research

I started this project with doing a brainstorm to narrow down what I wanted to create a site about. I knew I wanted to focus on the world of fashion which led me to my desk research where I delved into what my target audience is and where their interests lie within the world of fashion combined with what I felt there was a need for.

Moodboards & Styletile

Once I decied my site was going to be about fashion trendspotting, I made moodboards based upon the different countries I wanted my site to focus upon. The moodboard gives me insight to what I want to capture upon my site and helps me narrow down the design choices I will make towards my target audience. I made a styletile after which is the guidline to creating my website prototype and giving me a clear idea of its vision

Prototype & Wireframes

This was my first time appraoching wireframes and creating a prototype. I learned how the wireframes create the guideline I can carry onwards into my coding, whereas the prototype gives me the structured design of my site.

Key Takeaways

Throughout the coding process I had to learn how to use divâ€™s in HTML to organise my site into sections which I then can use in my CSS to create its layout and style. This project also challenged me more to broaden my knowledge within CSS

One of the challenges I faced was placing these circular links in a 2x2 grid. I had to go back and look at videos to understand once again the difference between display: flex and display: grid in order to achieve my desired layout.

RESULTS

Learnings & Solutions

The hardest part of this process was to find the correct ratio for the grids, both large and small, so that no image or text would overlap so I will take with me in my future projects that to have a set ratio/margin on the borders of my site and to practice more on grid structuring so that I am prepared to tackle them.

The general experience I also gained from this project was how to approach creating a website from both a designers angle and a developers angle as there were quite a few times throughout this project where I had to to sacrafice elements of my original prototype when coding as I was still in the early stages of learning how to develop a site. It taught me to apply more self discipline and rational thinking in the future when designing future websites as I have to remember skills I have and how far do I want to go in challenging myself to try new elements.

Overall, creating this website taught me how to combine design and coding to communicate fashion trends effectively. I learned to structure content clearly, use visuals to tell a story, and ensure the site is engaging and easy to navigate. This project helped me improve my coding skills while balancing creativity with functionality
