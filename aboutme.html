WHO AM I?

Hi, I'm Josephine! I'm a 21-year-old multimedia design student at KEA in Copenhagen. Growing up between cultures, being half Danish, half Dutch, and attending international schools in Germany has shaped my global outlook and creative perspective. Creativity has always been at the heart of who I am. From drawing and photography to scrapbooking and creating social media content, I've spent my life exploring different artistic outlets. Now, as I delve deeper into the world of multimedia design, I'm excited to combine my love for graphic design and content creation with the technical skills I'm learning at university.

A few fun facts about me: I'm a huge Harry Potter nerd, I can finish pretty much any line from any episode of Friends, and I absolutely cannot stand truffle. When I'm not immersed in design projects, you'll probably find me sketching, experimenting with new creative ideas, or curating my next social media project.
